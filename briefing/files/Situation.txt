After a refugee crisis, the political climate in Finland radicalized heavily. Seeing their chance, the Russian government started covertly supporting anti-government groups in Finland, managing to destabilize the government. A civil war broke out with the Russian-backed Finnish Protection Corps rebelling against government forces.
<br /> <br />
The FPC gained ground quickly due to the FDF not being prepared for action on this scale this quickly. Their advances have, however, halted, with the FDF managing to bring in the reserves. Frustrated at the stalemate and fearing the impending FDF counterattack, Russia has become increasingly uncautious in their covert support of the FPC, with most troops outside the reach of the press dropping the pretense finnishness entirely.
